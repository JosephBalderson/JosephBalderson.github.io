Mech 202: Engineering Design II

The engineering design process with emphasis on teamwork, ideation, decision-making, and project planning as applied to a group design project in mechanical engineering. Designed, built, and tested a projectile launcher that shot a cube, arrow, and ball accurately at a distance of 35, 75 and 105 feet. Developed a rapidly adjustable device that would accurately fire projectiles the required distances within the 3’x3’x3’ space requirement. Awarded First Place in the competition with over 20 teams.
